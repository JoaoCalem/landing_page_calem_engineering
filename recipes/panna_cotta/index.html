Gourmet Vanilla Panna Cotta

Optimized for a 10-hour timeline & professional texture

Total volume per serving: ~115ml

Ingredients (All Metric)

  • Heavy Cream (35% fat) 400g
  • Whole Milk
    OPTIONAL: Swap for Buttermilk for tang/balance
    280g
  • Granulated Sugar
    OPTIONAL: Toast at 150°C for 60m first
    70g
  • Vanilla Beans (Vahiné) 2 units
  • Silver Gelatin Sheets
    OR 7g powdered gelatin
    3 sheets
  • Kosher Salt Pinch

Method

1

Bloom Gelatin

Soak sheets in ice-cold water for 5-10 mins. If using powder, sprinkle over 2 tbsp cold water.

2

The Vanilla Grind

Scrape seeds from pods. Rub seeds aggressively into the (optionally toasted) sugar with your fingers until it looks like wet, aromatic sand.

3

Infuse Cream

Important:

Heat cream, vanilla sugar, pods, and salt until steaming. Remove from heat, cover, and steep for 20 mins. Do not add the buttermilk/milk yet.

4

Combine

Squeeze water from gelatin and whisk into warm cream. Slowly whisk in the cold milk or buttermilk.

5

The Suspension (Pro Step)

Strain mixture into a metal bowl set over an ice bath. Stir constantly until it thickens to a heavy syrup consistency. This prevents the vanilla seeds from sinking.

6

Set

Pour into glasses. Refrigerate immediately. Set time: 4-6 hours.

Enjoy your perfect dessert.
